Wanigaism

Introduction

Wanigaism is not a religion. A religion is a system of beliefs that have a central worshipped figure or supernatural power. Wanigaism as a concept is not a collective of beliefs around a supernatural being but rather a set of rules that any creature created by any god or power that worships or believes in any theory or ideal can follow to live their life to the best.
